--- layout: home paginate: true alt_title: "WISDOM" sub_title: "mm-Wave Interferometric Survey of Dark Object Masses" image: /assets/images/WISDOMbanner.png introduction: | The WISDOM (mm-Wave Interferometric Survey of Dark Object Masses) project is making very high resolution observations of nearby galaxy centres with several telescopes, including ALMA, CFHT and the VLA. We aim to: - Develop and calibrate the use of molecular gas as a tracer for measuring supermasive black hole masses - Use these advances to tie down SMBH-host relations across the Hubble sequence with a single technique. - Explore the extreme environments present in galaxy centres, and how they affect cloud statistics, star formation and gas flows in galaxy centers that fuel AGN Quick links: actions: - label: "Team" icon: Person url: "/team/" - label: "Papers" icon: Papers url: "/papers/" - label: "Codes" icon: github url: "https://github.com/TimothyADavis" - label: "Data" icon: download url: "/data/" ---
